Abstract

A vocabulary to define FAIR test results and their relationships with their tests, metrics, dimensions and benchmarks. This vocabulary builds on [W3C DCAT](https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-dcat-3/), [W3C PROV](https://www.w3.org/TR/prov-o/) and [W3C DQV](https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-dqv/) to represent all concepts and relationships.
